Instructions



 You will now play a game with other members of the class. Each person will guess a number from 0 to {{ session.config.endowment }}, with up to three decimal places. After everyone has submitted their number, we will compute the average (mean) guess. The person whose number is closest to two-thirds of this average wins the game.

 After entering your number, you will see the value of the average guess, two-thirds of the average guess, and whether or not you won the game.
