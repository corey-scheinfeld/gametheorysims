Instructions

In this game, you will randomly be matched with another player in the class. You will only interact via the computer, and will not know who you have been matched with. You and your partner must each simultaneously choose Movie or Concert simultaneously. The payoff you receive is determined by both your choice, and the choice of your partner, as described below. If the players choices mismatch, then each get a payoff of 0. If both choose Movie, then Row Player gets a payoff of 600 and Column gets a payoff of 200. If both choose Concert, then Row Player gets a payoff of 200 and Column gets a payoff of 600. You will play for 5 rounds, with 5 different partners
