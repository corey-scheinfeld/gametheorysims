Instructions

In this game, you will randomly be matched with another player in the class. You will only interact via the computer, and will not know who you have been matched with. You and your partner must each choose Hawk or Dove simultaneously. The payoff you receive is determined by both your choice, and the choice of your partner. You will play for 5 rounds, with 5 different partners.

Partner's Choice
Your Choice Hawk Dove
Hawk (-1, -1) (3, 0)
Dove (0, 3) (1.5, 1.5)
